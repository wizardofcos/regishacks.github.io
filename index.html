About

♦

Founded at Regis High School, one of the nation's most presitigious college preparatory institutions, RegisHacks aims to promote the advancement of high school students in technology-related fields. Designed to be educational as well as formative, the hackathon will focus on the exploration of potential careers by some of the best and brightest young minds today.
